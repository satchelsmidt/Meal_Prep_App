Meal Prepper
  • Logout

This app allows you to create a week-long meal plan based on your availability throughout the week, as well as the types of recipes you would like to cook for that week. Clicking 'Create Plan' will walk you through the plan creation process, while clicking 'View Plans' will allow you to view a list of the plans you have already created.
